KPBS Signature Generator

We are excited to introduce an easy-to-use tool that will help you create a professional email signature that aligns with our brand guidelines. With this signature generator, you can effortlessly include your contact information, preferred pronouns, social media handles, and a land acknowledgement statement. Plus, the current KPBS logo will be seamlessly integrated into your signature.

Our signature generator is designed to ensure consistency and accuracy across all electronic correspondence. Whether you're a new employee or a seasoned pro, this tool will make it easy to create a signature that reflects your role at KPBS and our shared commitment to excellence.

Get started today and elevate your email communication with a polished, on-brand signature!

Adding the signature to KPBS Email:

Visit the Google Create a Gmail signature Page for more details.
Or view the graphics below for tips on settings up the signature:

  1. Go to Your Gmail Settings by clicking on the Gear Icon
  2. Click "See All Settings"
  3. Click on Signature text field and paste in your Signature field.
    • To paste to the Mac: cmd + v.
    • To paste to the PC: Ctrl + v.
  4. Make sure that the following 2 fields are updated with your new Signature name. This will allow you to specify which email signature template will be used for new emails and reply/forwarding emails. View Infographic
    • “For New Emails Use”
    • “On Reply/Forward Use”

    Click "Save Changes" at the bottom to finish
